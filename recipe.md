First prepare all the Vegetables. Finely chop all the vegetables. 

prepare any utensil you are cooking in. 

add some water inside the utensil add the noodles let them boil a bit for about 5 minutes and keep stiring the noodles.

after that add a half spoon of oil in it so that hey don't stick together.

add the vegetables and 2 spoons of red chilli powder inside the noodles and  stir it agian and then cover it up with the lid. 

let it boil with the lid on. 

add salt as per your wish. 

And you instant Ramen Noodle is ready to eat.

now serve.
